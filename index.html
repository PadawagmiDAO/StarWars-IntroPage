Episode I

PadawanDAO

Over the last 10 years we've seen a revolution, a fight for decentralization. This fight has attracted intelligent beings from all across the galaxy.

During this battle, Ric Burton sponsored over 40 young and bright beings to attend Lisbon Blockchain Week 2021, effectively creating the Padawan society.

These young individuals came together to develop PadawanDAO, designed to be a fair system of deciding future sponsorships of young and broke beings. All funds generously donated are used to sponsor these beings.

By supporting PadawanDAO you are contributing to the next generation of fighters for decentralization.
